Our History

Happy Equipment Ltd have been and remains on top in the business of maintenance, servicing and repairs, including sales of all recommended generator engine type and equipments comprising Caterpillar, Marapco, Perkins F.G Wilson and Cummins, installing of all kinds of electrical panel and synchronization since the creation of the company in 2010. Happy Equipment Ltd is based in Port Harcourt Rivers State in Nigeria, the office address is No 202 Peter Odili Road, Portharcourt River State, Nigeria. Initially Happy Equipment Ltd capabilities were focused on Caterpillar generators and equipments maintenance, sales, service and repairs. However clients need some precipitated an expansion of these capabilities. Happy Equipment Ltd now engage in the business of sales, supply and purchase of other good brands of machineries and equipments. Few years of great creativity Happy Equipment Ltd in the past two years of its experience have focused on delivering high quality services to our clients. By thoughtful management we have maintained the development of our personnel from work force to management nurturing the continuity of their knowledge and experience, together with other good relationships with the clients. Today Happy Equipment Ltd is building on its foundation of care and thoroughness in its activities and maintaining a high level of creativity and innovation from research and development to make it a trade name in the industry.
